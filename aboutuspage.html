gh About Us

GH is a brand of interior products.

Our goal is to provide a diverse range of stylish and modern interior furnishings that are both high-quality and affordable. We aim to make exceptional design accessible to everyone.

From custom-built cabinetry to upholstered furniture and decorative pieces, the Geometry Home brand offers a complete cycle of furniture creation, ensuring a cohesive and elegant interior experience for all our customers.

HISTORY

The company was initially established as a trading company specializing in cushioned furniture

By 2011, shortly after starting sales, the company shifted its focus to both trade and production. Its own production facility was opened, enabling the formation of a demanded assortment, reduction of prices, and achievement of stable product quality.

In 2021, the company became the founder of the GH brand, whose mission is to provide a full range of interior products

In 2023, the company opened its own production facility for cushioned and cabinet furniture in Dubai.

By 2024, the company had already secured 7 contracts with major developers for wholesale furniture production for new construction projects.
